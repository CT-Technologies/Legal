Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

CT Technologies, LLC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application (AerScheduler RampOps) handles information.

Information We Collect

The AerScheduler RampOps app does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information or usage data on our servers. The app only stores an authentication token locally on your device to maintain your signed-in session.

How Information Is Used

The locally stored authentication token is used solely to verify your identity with the AerScheduler application. It is not shared with any third parties and is removed when you log out or uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

AerScheduler RampOps does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Security

Because no personal data is collected or stored on our servers, there is no risk of unauthorized access to user data. Authentication tokens remain securely stored on your device within app storage.

Your Control

You can clear the app’s local data or uninstall the app at any time to remove the stored authentication token.
